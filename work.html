Black Tech Mecca Idea Hack

Objective

To design and present a marketing plan and a new online platform for existing and potentially new members of the Black Tech Mecca community.

My Team

My team was comprised of five: two UX designers (myself included), one developer, one sales member, and one marketing member.

Challenges

We had 24 hours from beginning to end of project. Therefore, we were limited in user feedback.

Conditions

We worked in an agile, iterative environment over a short timeframe.

My contributions

I participated in the team sprints, wireframed the profile page and feeder pages, and interviewed about three available users regarding the low fidelity mock-ups, refining the features of the MVP. I also authored pieces of the Google slides that I presented.

Skills/Tools

  • Whiteboard and post-its for sprints
  • Balsamiq for low-fidelity mockups and wireframes
  • Google slides
  • Slack

Result

Black Tech Mecca will be applying elements of the various teams.

My Team

My team was comprised of four: one UX designer/project manager (me), one marketing member, and two sales members.

Challenges

Identifying the niche, value proposition and conveying this in the sales and marketing.

Conditions

My contributions
