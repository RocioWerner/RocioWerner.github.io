A Little Bit about Me

I am an innovation enthusiast and UX Designer, finding passion in process. I fell in love with design because it combines creativity with collaboration. Formerly, I was involved in process improvement projects for a private equity firm, aligning the user experience with process efficiency. An organizaton's mission and values matter to me, and thus, I took a leap to immerse myself in startup culture. I am also a dancer, which has provided me with the opportunities to collaborate with a diverse group of people. My other experiences include research science and retail. UX Design was a natural progression, bridging technology with design through passion, creativity and empathy.

My Style, My Values in My Work

Simple elegance is my design philosophy; the user is my priority. Whether in the startup world, a finance firm, or a dance performance, I have learnt that less is more, always empathetic to the user, client, or audience member. Iteration, iteration, iteration is my mantra. Equally important are hard work and fun through collaborative relationships.
