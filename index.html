About

Hey there. I am happy we found each other. Let's create an impactful user experience.


But first, who the heck am I? I am an innovation enthusiast and UX Designer, a multitasker who thrives in ambiguity. I fell in love with design because it combines creativity with collaboration. My recent projects have included work with startups. I especially enjoy designing for companies with an impactful mission as well as interesting mobile projects.


Formerly, I was involved in projects for a private equity firm, aligning the user experience with process efficiency. I collaborated closely with the IT Team to improve the UX for the internal transaction web interface and lead internal user testing. Prior to private equity, I was a scientific researcher, understading work hazards via hypothesis testing and ethnographic interviews.


When I'm not designing, I am presenting at events, networking, and dancing.
